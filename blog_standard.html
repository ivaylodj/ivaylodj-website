Welcome Message

W Welcome to my web corner. Hello and thank you for sparing your valuable time to visit my brand new humble amateur photographer website.

Idea and Mission

The general idea behind this site is to serve as my web domain presence corner, where I can showcase my best photographs from around the world and my home country - the beautiful Bulgaria.

The site main mission is to create a web platform where diverse individuals will enjoy, comment/give their feedback and inspire each other. Thsi will be achieved by providing a platform for showcasing, describe and give behind the scenes information.

Your personal feedback is invaluable for me and my progress as a photographer.

What you can find here:

  • My latest photographs and/or personal stories assoctaed with them.
  • Information about my personal projects, involving photography.
  • Information about my travels, involving photography.
  • Recent Blog posts.
  • Feedback opportunity.
  • My personal contacts.

Warning: Content copyright.

Please, be aware that all the photographs on this website are copyrighted and protected by the applicable law.

Content Downloading

You cannot download any photo content for personal and/or commercial use without the explicit written permission by the author i.e., by me. If, for some reason, you feel that you need one or more of the pictures and for example you need a more bigger image resolution - just drop me a line in the Contacts section of this website, so we can mutually arrange your request in the best possible way.

Content Sharing

You can share the content for personal use or if you are a non-commercial entity only via using the appropriate buttons alongside the content and if available. This action in turn will create a share-ready post for the most common social platforms like Facebook and/or Instagram. These share-ready posts are including my (author) details, which is mandatory when sharing.

You cannot share any content from this web site for commercial use or if you are a commercial entity/company etc., without the explicit written permission by the author i.e., by me. Please, contact me via Contacts section if you have such intentions.

How to use this site

You are free and encouraged to explore all the resources published on this web site. Feel free to comment on the photographs and/or the posts or to drop me a line for more information.

I encourage visitors to be:

  • Create comments/Give feedback with a clear purpose.
  • Be authentic.
  • Be respectful.
  • Be considerate.

What this site is not:

This site isn't a place to ask where and how to start with photography (of any kind/type). For this particluar purpose, there is an excellent photography hub in Bulgaria called photoschool.bg. I highly believe there is a similar in your home country, city or county as well. Just google for it and go ahead.

Need help with something?

Just drop me a line or few on my e-mail or do it directly from the Contacts section of this web site.

Personal information

Do not sell/resell my personal information, published in this website like my Names, address, e-mail, phone number etc. This information is provided just for the communication purposes. Any other use is illegal and prohibited by law.
