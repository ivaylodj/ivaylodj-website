FINE ART
PHOTOGRAPHY

A Visual Journey Through Light & Landscape

I'm Ivaylo Djounov, a fine art photographer exploring the world through the interplay of light, land, and time.

From the golden glow of sunrise over distant horizons to the profound silence of the night sky scattered with stars, my work captures the moments where light reveals the Earth's subtle beauty. I wander through landscapes—from the rugged cliffs of Europe to the vast African plains—seeking those fleeting moments when light transforms the ordinary into the extraordinary.

My photography spans multiple disciplines: the drama of nightscapes and astrophotography, where the Milky Way becomes a brushstroke across the darkness; the raw vitality of wildlife in their natural habitat; the ever-changing palette of seasonal transitions; and the intimate narratives of travel across continents and cultures.

Based in Varna, Bulgaria, I believe that exploration isn't merely a hobby—it's a meaningful quest to understand the world's visual language. Each photograph is a conversation between the eye and the landscape, a moment frozen in time.

Wander through my galleries and discover journeys across continents, beneath starlit skies, and into the heart of nature's most compelling moments. Your curiosity is welcome here.

Connect with me on Instagram and Facebook to follow my latest expeditions into light and landscape.
