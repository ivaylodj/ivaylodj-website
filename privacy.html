Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 July 2026

This website, ivaylodj.com, is a personal fine-art photography site run by Ivaylo Djounov (Varna, Bulgaria). This policy explains what personal data the site collects, why, and the rights you have over it under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Questions? Email hello@ivaylodj.com.

1. Blog comments (Google sign-in)

To leave a comment on a blog post you sign in with your Google account. When you do, and when you post, the site stores:

  • your name and profile photo (avatar) as provided by Google;
  • a Google-issued account identifier (so you can edit or delete your own comments);
  • the sign-in provider (e.g. "google");
  • your comment text and the date/time it was posted or edited.

Your name, avatar and comment are shown publicly next to your comment on the post. We do not receive or store your Google password. The legal basis is your consent, given when you choose to sign in and post.

You stay in control: while signed in you can edit or delete your own comments at any time. Deleting a comment removes it permanently (deleting a top-level comment also removes its replies).

2. Comment notification emails

When a comment is posted, an automated email is sent to the site owner so it can be reviewed. Only the site owner receives this email; it is not shared with anyone else.

3. Analytics & hosting

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. It uses Cloudflare Web Analytics (privacy-friendly, no cookies) for aggregate visitor statistics, and Google Tag Manager, which may load Google analytics tools that set cookies to measure traffic. These tools help understand how the site is used; they are not used to build advertising profiles.

4. Cookies

A sign-in session cookie (from Google / our authentication provider) keeps you logged in while commenting. Analytics tools described above may set their own cookies. You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings; the site works without them, except that you won't be able to sign in to comment.

5. Where your data is processed

Comment data is stored with Supabase on servers in the EU (Frankfurt, Germany). Sign-in is handled by Google, notification email by Resend, and hosting/analytics by Cloudflare and Google. These providers act as processors and have their own privacy policies.

6. How long we keep it

Comments (and the associated name, avatar link, provider and identifier) are kept until you delete them or the site owner removes them. Aggregate analytics are retained by the analytics providers per their policies.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and object to or restrict its processing. Many of these you can do yourself by editing or deleting your comments. For anything else, email hello@ivaylodj.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority — in Bulgaria, the Commission for Personal Data Protection (CPDP).

8. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time; the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.
