Capturing Light, Exploring Worlds

I'm a fine art photographer exploring the intersection of light, landscape, and time. Every photograph is a conversation between the eye and the world—from the profound silence of starlit skies to the raw drama of distant continents.

Based in Varna, Bulgaria, I wander through landscapes seeking those fleeting moments where light transforms the ordinary into the extraordinary.

Get In Touch
Landscape

The interplay of light and land. I seek compositions where geography becomes visual language—from the architectural drama of coastal cliffs to the subtle layers of mountain valleys.

Travel

Photography across continents. From Morocco's ochre villages to Namibia's infinite dunes, travel reveals how geography shapes stories—capturing landscapes and the narratives embedded within them.

Wildlife

Animals in their element. Whether a bird in flight or a creature in its habitat, wildlife photography captures the raw vitality and authenticity of nature—unscripted, immediate, alive.

Nightscape

The cosmos as landscape. Long exposures reveal what the naked eye cannot—the Milky Way's brushstrokes across darkness, star constellations emerging from seemingly black skies.

The Disciplines That Drive My Work

My photography spans multiple disciplines, each exploring a different facet of light, time, and place.

Landscape & The Elements

Seeking the moments where geography becomes a visual poem. Whether coastal drama or mountain silence, I photograph places where light reveals what makes them feel.

Nightscape & Astrophotography

Beyond the visible spectrum. Long exposures transform seemingly dark skies into star-scattered tapestries. The night sky is one of Earth's final truly wild frontiers.

Let's Collaborate

If my work resonates with you, I'd love to connect. Whether it's a commission, collaboration, or simply to share photography insights, I'm always interested in conversations with fellow explorers.

Background

Ivaylo Djounov has been photographing light and landscape for around six years, based in Varna on Bulgaria's Black Sea coast. His work focuses on nightscapes and astrophotography, landscape, and travel.

Gear: Sony A7 III & Sony A7 IV; Sony 14mm f/1.8, 24mm f/1.4, 24–70mm f/2.8 GM II, 70–200mm f/2.8 GM II, 100–400mm f/3.5–5.6 GM, 200–600mm f/5.6–6.3 G, and a 1.4× teleconverter.

Available for: prints, licensing & collaborations. Get in touch.
