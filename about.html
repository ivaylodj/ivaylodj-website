Landscape

Capture the beauty of nature, bringing the viewers into the scenery, setting, and mood in these outdoor locations. Landscape photography captures the beauty of the great outdoors.

Travel

Travel photography is a genre of photography that may involve the documentation of an area's landscape, people, cultures, customs, and history.

Wildlife

Wildlife photography is a genre of photography concerned with documenting various forms of wildlife in their natural habitat.

Nightscape

Capture a landscape style image, at night. It can include the beautiful Milky Way, a starry sky, or star constellation. This is a type of astrophotography, where long exposure images are taken to reveal the beautiful light of a seemingly ‘dark’ sky.
